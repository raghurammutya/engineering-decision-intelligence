# Product Vision Convergence Notes: 2026-05-23

## Core Breakthrough

The product is not an artifact governance platform.

The product is a continuous engineering reality reconciliation system:

> Observed engineering reality no longer matches intended operating policy.

This is the heart of the platform.

## Strategic Pivot

Old framing:

- artifact inventory,
- metadata registry,
- static governance,
- dashboard-first reporting.

New framing:

- continuously synchronized engineering reality,
- intended versus observed reconciliation,
- drift and risk detection,
- evidence-backed decisions,
- generated materialized views.

## Product Spine

Every feature should pass this test:

> Does this help continuously reconcile intended engineering policy with
> observed operational reality?

If yes, build or consider it. If no, defer it.

## First MVP

Operational State Mutation Reconciliation:

> Which workflows, scripts, agents, jobs, or automations can change operational
> state, and are they canonical, owner-approved, evidenced, and safe?

## Critical Architecture Decisions

- The graph is closer to truth than generated reports.
- Registries are materialized views, not primary storage.
- Governance is adaptive and risk-based.
- AI is advisory before it is authoritative.
- Divergence is detected first, judged second.
- The platform must govern itself.
