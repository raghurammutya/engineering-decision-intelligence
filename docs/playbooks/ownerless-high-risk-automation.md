# Ownerless High-Risk Automation

## Trigger

A high-risk workflow or script has no owner boundary.

## Standard Remediation

1. Assign an accountable owner or service boundary.
2. Record the owner in policy.
3. Confirm evidence and rollback expectations.
4. Re-run policy coverage.

## Completion Evidence

- owner map entry,
- reviewed next action,
- evidence status updated.
